✦ about me ✦

hi, i'm rae!

welcome to pretty kitty place, a little corner of the internet for everything cute, kawaii, and sticker-shaped. i design all of my own original characters and bring them to life as high-quality stickers, perfect for your laptop, water bottle, planner, or anywhere else that needs a little more cute.

every sticker is made with love and shipped with care. come hang out on my socials to see new drops, behind-the-scenes, and way too many cat pics.

meet tiple!

tiple is the star of pretty kitty place. he's a gray cat and my very own original character, inspired by my two real cats maple and tinker bell. i took the best parts of both of them, combined them together, and tiple was born!
