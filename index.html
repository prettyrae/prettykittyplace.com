✦ about me ✦

hi, i'm rae!

welcome to pretty kitty place — my little corner of the vendor world! i make handmade keychains and magnets featuring original characters and designs, and i vend at pokémon card shows where i also bring cards to trade and sell.

every piece i make is designed by me and made with love. come find my table at a show near you — i'd love to meet you! 🩷
